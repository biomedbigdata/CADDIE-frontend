Documentation

Concept

CADDIE (Cancer Driver Drug interaction explorer) repurposes drugs in cancer based on gene-gene and drug-gene interaction networks. The user has the option to choose between the implemented interaction networks from STRING, BioGRID, DrugBank, ChEMBL and more. CADDIE then exploits the information in selected networks using network algorithms like Trustrank or Degree Centrality. The algorithms and the desired parameters are again customizable by the user. Doing this, CADDIE is able to identify drug repurposing candidates or to search for putative drug targets.

Explorer

The Explorer is the main part of the application. It allows you to explore the interactome of you cancer type or cancer driver genes of interest. Most importantly, it gives you a variety of information like comorbidities or expression values to help you select seed genes for your drug or drug-target search.
On the left side you can find a menu bar which gives you the options to select your cancer type or gene interaction resource of interest. You will receive a short overview over your cancer type of interest. After selecting your seed genes, the left menu allows you to start analysis tasks (max. 3 at a time). The tasks will be displayed at the bottom of this menu in the 'Tasks' section. A click on a finished task leads you to the analysis view.
On the right side of the explorer is the gene-interaction network. It contains all cancer driver genes of your selected cancer type, forming a minimal spanning tree. It is supplemented by genes found by the multiple steiner tree algorithm in order to make the minimal spanning tree possible. You can add your own genes to the network by uploading your own seed genes (for example via the 'Custom genes' function in the left menu) followed by the 'Add to network' functionality. Gene-gene interactions are taken from the selected interaction database.

Analysis View

THe analysis view opens when you click on a finished task in the left menu in the explorer. The task results are shown in a network and as a table. The network displays all seed genes in combination with the found genes and/or drugs and allows you to inspect the relations from the seeds to the results. The table view shows you more detail information about the genes and drugs. Depending on the selected algorithm, a score is assigned to each of the genes/drugs.
In addition, depending of the type of the task, one or more figures allow an overview over the task result.

Drug Interaction Lookup

The Drug Interaction Lookup enables you to inspect the interaction datasets in respect to individual drugs. If the searched drug is not found in the database, the drug with the closest matching name will be shown.
All interacting genes based on the selected drug-gene interaction dataset will be listed. Cancer driver genes for any cancer type in the CADDIE database will be highlighted.
